ADVOCACY

Advocacy

Baldiabassa Gramin Unnayan Samity advocacy initiatives aim to influence existing and upcoming policies to uphold, scale and sustain the empowerment of people from the most marginalized communities.

Baldiabassa Gramin Unnayan Samity engages with policymakers and builds relationships with key State and State level networks and alliances to build support for the marginalized communities. Building on the work done over the past few years, the Baldiabassa Gramin Unnayan Samity focuses on developing outcome-based thematic advocacy strategies for all the sectors Baldiabassa Gramin Unnayan Samity operates in. At the international level, Baldiabassa Gramin Unnayan Samity engages with various processes to advocate on issues relevant to India. The organization also conducts studies and develops knowledge products to advocate on various issues affecting the marginalized communities.

In FY 2015-16, Baldiabassa Gramin Unnayan Samity was consulted for several State and National level policy-related work by the Government of India and was invited by various State Governments for suggestions on the implementation of various development programs. Baldiabassa Gramin Unnayan Samity also participated and contributed in important civil society and government processes related to Sustainable Development Goals, both within the country and outside.
